How to Play

Press start to start playing. At the beginning of the game the game will give you three cards and three to your opponent each round you will have to choose one of the powers which are:


  • Power
  • speed
  • Stamina

The card with the highest number will win.


Each round is worth one point, there are twenty-one rounds. Every three rounds the game will give you three new cards.


The Winner will be the one with the highest number of points.
